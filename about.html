Frequently Asked Questions

Sassy is known for their pop rock, classic, jazzy playing style. They've come up to this genre with the influence of the bands December Avenue, Oh! Caraga & Rob Deniel. The band was formed last August 2022.

The most popular song of the band was their very 1st single, ECQ (E-Crushback Ko). It caught the attention of many teens in Surigao City. A simple song yet gives a charm and nostalgic moment whoever listens with the song

The band was originally a 5-piece band. It consist of Lyndon on vocals, Yanyan on lead guitars, Andy on guitars, Reu on bass and Christian playing drums. Late October of 2022 when Jm Escuredo joined the band. Late January 2023 when Yanyan, their original lead guitarist left the band. It was early March when Troy joined the band to complete their line up.

The band plays a variety of songs mostly focus on the pop rock genre. Their known for their visayan songs, radio friendly and easy to listen type of songs. They cover songs from some of their influence bands.
