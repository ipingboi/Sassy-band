Sassy Band

A Pop rock band based in Surigao City. Ignited since 2022, playing at schools as guests, big stages & band of the bands. Known for their pop rock, classic, jazzy playing style style. Their very 1st single, ECQ was released last January 13, 2023 and caught the attention of many teens in the City. Now, they've already released 3 of their original songs and stillworking hard to release their 1st album this 2023.
